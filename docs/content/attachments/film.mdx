Film rolls store captured photos.\
Currently, there are 2 types of film: **Color** and **Black and White**. These are pretty self-explanatory.\
Film roll can store up to 16 frames.

<Callout>
    *Film does not store actual images, but **information** about the shot you took. This is not a real world after all.*
</Callout>
